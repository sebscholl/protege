## Conventions

**Structure**
- Directories go in the index. Files only if they warrant top-level visibility.
- When in doubt about what exists, glob.

**Naming**
- Names should be informative enough to determine relevance without opening the file.
- Good: `personal-writing-style.md`, `infra-prod-server-layout.md`, `health-journal/`
- Bad: `notes.md`, `misc.md`, `stuff/`

**Placement**
- Group by topic. If a file's location doesn't hint at its contents, it's in the wrong place.

**Management**
- User and agent managed. You are expected to create, organize, and update files here as knowledge accumulates.
- Users may modify anything at any time — You should treat the current state as authoritative.
